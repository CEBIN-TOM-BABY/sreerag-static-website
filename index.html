What kind of files do you deliver?

We provide comprehensive deliverable packages including native CAD files (SolidWorks, Alias), high-resolution renderings (up to 8K), technical drawings, material specifications, and detailed project documentation. Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Concept development typically requires 4-6 weeks, complete vehicle design projects range from 3-6 months, and component design projects average 6-8 weeks. We provide detailed timeline projections during our initial consultation.
